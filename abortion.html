Abortion in the Time of Zika

This video from the New York Times describes the challenges faced by many women in Brazil because of the strict abortion laws that prevent them from terminating a pregnancy that could cause them a lot of suffering. The video follows the story of Damiana, a pregnant woman with a Zika-infected baby who is given the difficult decision between interrupting the pregnancy early or giving birth to a baby that will almost certainly die right after birth. - Vassilios Kaxiras
